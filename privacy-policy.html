Mark Buddy Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2024

Introduction

Mark Buddy is a Chrome extension designed to help you organize and manage your bookmarks with smart categories. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data and what information we collect.

Information We Collect

Mark Buddy collects and stores the following information:

  • Your bookmarks (URLs, titles, and organization)
  • Custom categories you create
  • Bookmark-category associations

How We Use Your Information

All information collected by Mark Buddy is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To provide bookmark management functionality
  • To organize your bookmarks into categories
  • To maintain your bookmark preferences and settings

Data Storage

All your data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API. We do not:

  • Send your data to any external servers
  • Share your data with third parties
  • Use your data for any purpose other than providing the extension's functionality

Permissions Used

Mark Buddy requires the following permissions:

  • bookmarks: To create, read, and manage your bookmarks
  • storage: To save your categories and preferences
  • activeTab: To access the current tab's information when bookmarking
  • contextMenus: To provide right-click menu options for quick bookmarking

Data Security

Your data security is important to us. We implement the following measures:

  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • No data is transmitted over the internet
  • We use Chrome's built-in security features for data storage

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • You can uninstall the extension at any time
  • You can clear all stored data through Chrome's extension settings
  • You can export your bookmark data using the extension's export feature

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the Chrome Web Store listing.
